Morven MacKenzie
TimePeace

'TimePeace' personalises and privatises time, only revealing itself when and where the recipient is ready to gauge and engage. The process is itself holistic: the journey towards the object and touch-responsive mechanism constituting a reflection on how much we need our experience regimented through time. The object, cool to the touch and identifiable as a soothing, mantlepiece clock has a calm, quiet face.

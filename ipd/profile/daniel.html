Twitter Calender

TwitterCalender is designed to help people arrange their daily routines. Users just send "time+thing" to twitter status and the calendar will download the information and display on a LCD which surrounded by a series of leds. It aims to remind users when and what to do. Leds give different colors depending on the time from messages. Red means emergency, yellow means less urgent and green means safe, so users may react respectively on the colors.

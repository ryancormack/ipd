Music Echo is designed for people with hearing impairments.
The Music Echo is a new product designed for people with hearing impairments, to enhance their enjoyment of music. The Music Echo takes any song and clarifies it by vibrating the music through a range of interactive beads. Simply wrapping the beads around your hand or putting them in your pocket will create a new dimension to the music. The portable and stylish design allows the user to proudly wear the Music Echo in any situation.

Connect a Sketch

Connect A Sketch creates a two way connection between children to communicate through the classic toy. Log onto the Connect A Sketch website with your unique user ID. On here you will be able to wirelessly link your device and choose who you want to talk to. Both devices draw simultaneously, allowing friends to collaborate on drawings. In keeping the original interactions of the Etch A Sketch, when one user rubs out a sketch, the connected drawing machine will shake alerting the second user to rub their drawings out.They will repeat this action to confirm that the canvas has been cleared.

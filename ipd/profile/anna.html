HejHej

HejHej is a communication device for internet telephony such as Skype. The device is designed to be wirelessly connected to your home pc allowing you to have a conversation anywhere in your house. May it be cooking together in the kitchen, hanging out in the living room, or, why not share the sound of birds tweeting in your garden? The intuitive design of HejHej allows us to use modern technology in a natural way. HejHej is about sharing moments and daily life with your loved ones whilst apart.

Salus

salus is a mountaineering safety tool for concise communication between parties of climbers or individuals. Salus uses coloured lights as basic commands to create a conversation.  Each unit has four buttons which represent commands and an acknowledgment button. The conversation begins when a button is pressed, this will send a signal to the partnered device illuminating its coloured light. The climber can then reply by pressing acknowledge. A simple sequence of commands can be achieved. Climber – "safe," Belayer  - " acknowledgement," Climber -  "on belay," Belayer  - "acknowledgement," Climber – "climb on" salus is intended for mountaineering and multi pitch climbing use.
